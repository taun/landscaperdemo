Summary

  • Every fractal is defined by a set of drawing rules, angle, color and thickness.
  • Fractal angles and thickness and some colors can be changed by dragging your finger on the screen.
  • Pop-up the editor to change the colors, effects and rules using press-hold-drag-drop.
  • Copy a fractal before changing it if you want to create a new different fractal.
  • Export fractals to show friends your creation. In-app purchase for exporting as PDF vector and to remove the FractalScapes watermark.
  • Share fractals to the cloud for others to appreciate, download and edit. In-app purchase required for cloud sharing but not to download from the FractalScapes cloud.
